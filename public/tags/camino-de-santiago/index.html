9: An Ode to the Road

Posted on December 21, 2017 in Camino de Santiago

Each and every day I wake and walk with the sun. It starts low, climbing over hillsides, rising slowly. It finds its temporary place amid the sky and begins to color the Meseta. Front and center a shadow grows. It looks at me with indifference – a mirror of my darker side. Fear, insecurity, doubt, The manifestation of my inner demons. Each and every day, I wake and walk and confront them.
